Unity – Planetary Escape

Role: Gameplay Designer (Solo) · Platform: PC

The development of this project was designed to simulate a professional prototyping environment, with a focus on timeboxing, scope management and rapid iteration.
I defined the core gameplay concept and developed a playable prototype within a set time constraint, using playtesting to guide development.
This strengthened my ability to prioritize features, manage scope, and refine systems based on feedback.

  • Design focus: Prototyping, Testing, Iteration
  • Constraints: Fixed development time, no visual machine.
  • Results: Conducted informal playtests, Observed player engagement and pacing

Unity – Parkour Master

Role: Gameplay Designer (Solo) · Platform: PC

A 2D platformer focused on game feel and player feedback, exploring how moment-to-moment interactions shape the overall player experience.
I designed and refined player movement, responsiveness, and feedback systems to improve clarity and player control, prioritizing how actions feel rather than just how they function.
This project strengthened my understanding of how small design changes can significantly impact player experience and engagement.

  • Design focus: Game feel, Juice, Impact and player feedback
  • Design Decisions: Prioritized feedback that reinforced player actions
